## Key Features Demonstrated

### Meeting States
1. **Live Meeting**: Real-time indicator with pulsing red dot
2. **AI Processing**: Progress bar showing percentage (65%)
3. **AI Processed**: Complete summaries with sentiment
4. **Upcoming**: Countdown timers and prep tips
5. **No Recording**: Special handling for meetings without recordings

### Smart Details
- **HRMS Connection**: TechStart and DataFlow show HRMS badges
- **Recording Status**: Shows "Not available" for StartCo (no recording)
- **Transcript Sources**: Shows "(from notes)" for manual transcripts
- **Time Display**:
  - Live: "Started 15 minutes ago"
  - Upcoming: "In 2 hours" or "Tomorrow 10:00 AM"
  - Completed: Full date and time range

### Action Buttons
- **Live**: "Join Meeting" + "View Live Transcript"
- **Upcoming**: "Join Early" + "View Prep Notes" + "Reschedule"
- **In-Person Upcoming**: "View Details" + "View Prep Notes" + "Get Directions"
- **Processing**: "View Details" + "Wait for AI Summary" (disabled)
- **Completed with Recording**: "View Details" + "Play Recording" + "View Transcript"
- **Completed without Recording**: "View Details" + "View Transcript"

### Filtering Capabilities
All filters work in real-time:
- **Time Range**: All, Today, This Week, This Month, Custom
- **Status**: All, Upcoming, Completed, Live
- **Type**: All, Call, Video, In-Person
- **AI Status**: All, Processed, Processing, Not Recorded
- **Search**: By meeting title, attendee name, or deal name
