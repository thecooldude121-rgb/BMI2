## 🎨 COLOR CODING

**Signal Types:**
- 💰 Funding = Green background (bg-green-50)
- 📈 Hiring = Blue background (bg-blue-50)
- 🚀 Product = Pink background (bg-pink-50)
- 🌍 Expansion = Orange background (bg-orange-50)

**Status Badges:**
- 🟢 New Signal = Green text
- 🟡 In Review = Yellow text
- ✅ Converted = Blue text
- ❌ Dismissed = Red text

**Action Buttons:**
- Primary (Add/View Lead) = Blue bg-blue-600
- Secondary (View Details) = White with border
- Dismiss = White with border
- Undo = Orange bg-orange-600

## 🔍 WHAT TO LOOK FOR

### ✅ GOOD SIGNS:
- All buttons have hover effects
- Modals center on screen
- Navigation works instantly
- Filters update the view
- Stats cards have subtle shadow on hover
- Menu closes when clicking outside

### ❌ RED FLAGS:
- Broken navigation (404 errors)
- Modals don't open
- Buttons don't respond to clicks
- Filters don't change displayed signals
- Console errors
- Styling issues
